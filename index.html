MEAT ME INSIDE

Rated as one of the best steakhouses in Manila, Meat Me Inside currently has twenty-one locations worldwide. We pride ourselves in our renowned USDA Prime Black Angus Steak, fresh seafood, and award-winning wine cellar.

BESTSELLERS

Ribeye 12oz & Grilled Shrimp

A thick, juicy 12oz ribeye, perfectly seared for a caramelized crust and bursting with rich, buttery flavor. Paired with smoky, flame-grilled shrimp brushed with garlic butter, this surf-and-turf masterpiece melts in your mouth with every bite.

Bacon Cheeseburger

A juicy, flame-grilled beef patty dripping with savory goodness, topped with crispy, smoky bacon and oozing melted cheddar. Sandwiched between a toasted brioche bun, every bite is a perfect balance of crunch, creaminess, and pure burger bliss.

Grilled Salmon

Tender, flaky salmon fillet kissed by the flames, with a crispy, golden crust and a melt-in-your-mouth center. Brushed with buttery herbs and a hint of citrus, every bite is a perfect blend of smoky, savory, and fresh flavors.
